Machine Learning Demonstration

  • First, enable the webcam and allow access when prompted.
  • Find an object and point the camera at it. Press the "Gather class 1 data" button to start collecting approximately 50 samples. Press the button again to finish gathering the data.
  • Repeat the process for class 2 using a different object of interest. Collect a similar number of samples.
  • Click the "Train & Predict" button and wait for the model to train live in your browser. This may take around 1 minute.
  • Once trained, you will see live predictions appear at the bottom of the page for what it thinks it sees.
